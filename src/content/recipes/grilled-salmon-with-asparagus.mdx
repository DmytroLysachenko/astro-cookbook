## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Salmon and Asparagus

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. While the grill is heating, season the salmon fillets with **salt, black pepper, and minced garlic**. Drizzle with **1 tablespoon of olive oil** and set aside.

Trim the tough ends off the asparagus and toss them with the remaining **1 tablespoon of olive oil**, salt, and pepper.

<CookingStep action="seasoning-salmon" />

### Step 2: Grill the Salmon

Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin-side down. Grill for **4-5 minutes** on each side, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.  
-Tip:- Avoid flipping the salmon too early to prevent it from sticking to the grill.

<CookingStep action="grilling-fish-red" />

### Step 3: Grill the Asparagus

While the salmon is cooking, place the asparagus on the grill. Grill for **3-4 minutes**, turning occasionally, until tender and slightly charred.  
-Tip:- You can also use a grill basket to make flipping the asparagus easier.

<CookingStep action="grilling-asparagus" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Plate the grilled salmon and asparagus together. Squeeze fresh **lemon juice** over the top for a burst of citrus flavor. Serve immediately and enjoy!
