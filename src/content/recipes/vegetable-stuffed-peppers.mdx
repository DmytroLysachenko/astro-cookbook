## Introduction

**Vegetable Stuffed Peppers** are a **healthy and colorful dish** that’s perfect for a vegetarian dinner. Made with bell peppers stuffed with a mixture of vegetables, rice, and herbs, this dish is both nutritious and delicious.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Peppers

Cut the tops off the **bell peppers** and remove the seeds and membranes. Set aside.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-vegetables-sweetPepper"
  alt="Preparing bell peppers for stuffing"
/>

### Step 2: Cook the Rice

Cook the **brown rice** according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

<CookingStep action="boiling-grains-rice" alt="Boiling brown rice in a pot" />

### Step 3: Prepare the Filling

Chop the **zucchini, tomatoes, onion, and garlic**. In a pan, heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add the **onion and garlic** and sauté until softened. Add the **zucchini and tomatoes** and cook until tender. Stir in the cooked **rice, basil, salt, and pepper**.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-pan-mix-vegetableMix"
  alt="Sautéing vegetables for stuffing"
/>

### Step 4: Stuff the Peppers

Fill the **bell peppers** with the vegetable and rice mixture. Sprinkle with **Parmesan cheese**.

<CookingStep action="stuffed-peppers" alt="Stuffed peppers ready for baking" />

### Step 5: Bake and Serve

Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and bake at 180°C (350°F) for **30 minutes**, or until the peppers are tender. Serve hot.

<CookingStep
  action="kitchenAppliances-oven"
  alt="Baking stuffed peppers in the oven"
/>

These **Vegetable Stuffed Peppers** are a **nutritious and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of fresh vegetables, rice, and herbs makes this dish a family favorite. Enjoy!
