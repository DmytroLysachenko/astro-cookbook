## Introduction

**Chicken Tikka Masala** is a **flavorful Indian dish** that combines marinated chicken with a creamy tomato sauce. Served with rice or naan, this dish is a crowd-pleaser.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, mix the **chicken breast** with **yogurt, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, salt, and pepper**. Let it marinate for at least **30 minutes**.

<CookingStep
  action="marinating-meat-chicken-breast"
  alt="Marinating chicken in a bowl"
/>

### Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Heat a pan over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-pan-meat-poultry-breast"
  alt="Cooking chicken in a pan"
/>

### Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

In the same pan, sauté the **onion, garlic, and ginger** until softened. Add the **tomato puree** and cook for a few minutes. Stir in the **heavy cream** and bring to a simmer.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring-saucePan-tomato-onion"
  alt="Stirring tomato sauce in a pan"
/>

### Step 4: Combine and Serve

Return the chicken to the pan and simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened. Serve hot with rice or naan.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-chicken-sauce"
  alt="Stirring chicken in tomato sauce"
/>

## Final Thoughts

This **Chicken Tikka Masala** is a **rich and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of spices and creamy tomato sauce makes this dish a favorite. Enjoy!
