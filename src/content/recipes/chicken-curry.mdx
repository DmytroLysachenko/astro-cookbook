## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Chicken

Cut the **chicken breast** into bite-sized pieces. Season with **salt** and **pepper**.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-meat-poultry-breasts-bites"
  alt="Cutting chicken breast"
/>

### Step 2: Cook the Aromatics

In a large pan, heat **oil** and sauté **chopped onions**, **minced garlic**, and **grated ginger** until softened.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-vegetables-onions" alt="Cooking aromatics" />

### Step 3: Add the Spices

Add **curry powder**, **turmeric**, and **cumin** to the pan. Stir for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

<CookingStep action="seasoning-spices-curry" alt="Adding spices" />

### Step 4: Cook the Chicken

Add the **chicken pieces** to the pan and cook until browned on all sides. Stir in **tomato puree** and **coconut milk**. Simmer for 20 minutes.

<CookingStep action="simmer-chicken-curry" alt="Cooking chicken curry" />

### Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the chicken curry hot with **rice** or **naan bread**. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired.

## Final Thoughts

Chicken Curry is a **rich and aromatic dish** that’s perfect for a comforting dinner. The combination of **chicken, spices, and creamy sauce** creates a satisfying dish. Enjoy this international recipe with your family and friends!
