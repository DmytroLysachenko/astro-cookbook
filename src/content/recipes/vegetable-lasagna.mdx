## Introduction

**Vegetable Lasagna** is a **hearty and healthy dish** that’s perfect for a vegetarian dinner. Made with layers of pasta, fresh vegetables, and a creamy béchamel sauce, this lasagna is both delicious and nutritious.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Slice the **zucchini, eggplant, and tomatoes** into thin slices. Sauté the **garlic** in **olive oil** until fragrant, then add the vegetables and cook until softened.

<CookingStep
  action="frying_pan_mix_vegetableMix"
  alt="Sautéing vegetables in a pan"
/>

### Step 2: Prepare the Béchamel Sauce

In a saucepan, melt **butter** and whisk in **flour** to create a roux. Gradually add **milk** while whisking continuously until the sauce thickens. Season with **salt, pepper, and nutmeg**.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring_saucePan_cream"
  alt="Stirring béchamel sauce in a saucepan"
/>

### Step 3: Assemble the Lasagna

In a baking dish, layer the **lasagna sheets, vegetables, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella cheese**. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel sauce and a sprinkle of **Parmesan cheese**.

<CookingStep
  action="baking_oven_savory_lasagna"
  alt="Assembling lasagna in a baking dish"
/>

### Step 4: Bake and Serve

Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for **30-40 minutes**, or until the top is golden and bubbly. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

## Final Thoughts

This **Vegetable Lasagna** is a **delicious and healthy alternative** to traditional meat lasagna. The layers of pasta, fresh vegetables, and creamy béchamel sauce make this dish a family favorite. Enjoy!
