## Introduction

Chicken Enchiladas are a **classic Mexican dish** that features shredded chicken wrapped in corn tortillas, topped with a flavorful enchilada sauce and melted cheese. This dish is **perfect for a family dinner** and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Cook the Chicken

Season the chicken breasts with **salt, cumin, and chili powder**. Cook in a pan until fully cooked, then shred the chicken using two forks.

<CookingStep action="frying_pan_meat_poultry_breast" />

### Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with **chopped onion, minced garlic, and half of the enchilada sauce**.

<CookingStep action="mixing_bowl_vegetables_stirFry" />

### Step 3: Assemble the Enchiladas

Preheat the oven to **180°C (350°F)**. Fill each corn tortilla with the chicken mixture, roll them up, and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.

<CookingStep action="baking_oven_savory_casserole" />

### Step 4: Top with Sauce and Cheese

Pour the remaining enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas and sprinkle with **grated cheddar cheese**.

<CookingStep action="seasoning_spices_mix" />

### Step 5: Bake the Enchiladas

Bake in the preheated oven for **20-25 minutes**, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

<CookingStep action="baking_oven_savory_lasagna" />

### Step 6: Garnish and Serve

Garnish the enchiladas with **chopped cilantro** and serve with **sour cream** and **guacamole**.

## Final Thoughts

Chicken Enchiladas are a **delicious and comforting dish** that’s perfect for a family dinner. The combination of **spicy chicken, tangy sauce, and melted cheese** makes this dish a favorite among Mexican cuisine lovers. Enjoy this flavorful meal with your family and friends!
