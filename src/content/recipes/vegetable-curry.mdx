## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Chop the **potatoes, carrots, cauliflower, and green beans** into bite-sized pieces. Dice the **onion** and mince the **garlic and ginger**.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-vegetables-mix-tomato-califlower"
  alt="Preparing vegetables for curry"
/>

### Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

In a large pot, heat **oil** over medium heat. Add the **onion, garlic, and ginger** and sauté until softened. Stir in the **curry powder and turmeric** and cook for another minute.

<CookingStep
  action="pan-vegetables-mix"
  alt="Sautéing onions and spices in a pot"
/>

### Step 3: Cook the Vegetables

Add the **potatoes, carrots, cauliflower, and green beans** to the pot. Stir to coat the vegetables with the spices. Pour in the **coconut milk** and **tomato puree** and bring to a simmer. Cook until the vegetables are tender.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring-soupPot-curry-soup"
  alt="Stirring vegetable curry in a pot"
/>

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Season the curry with **salt and pepper**. Serve hot with rice or naan.

## Final Thoughts

This **Vegetable Curry** is a **nutritious and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of fresh vegetables and a rich coconut milk sauce makes this curry a family favorite. Enjoy!
