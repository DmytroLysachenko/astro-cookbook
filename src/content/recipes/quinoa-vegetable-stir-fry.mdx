## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Cook the **quinoa** according to package instructions. Chop the **broccoli**, **carrots**, and **bell pepper**. Mince the **garlic**.

<CookingStep action="chopping-carrot-broccoli" />

### Step 2: Cook the Stir-Fry

Heat the **olive oil** in a pan and sauté the **garlic** until fragrant. Add the **broccoli**, **carrots**, and **bell pepper**, and stir-fry until tender. Add the cooked **quinoa** and **soy sauce**, and stir to combine. Season with **salt** and **black pepper**.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-mix-vegetableMix" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the stir-fry hot as a main dish or side.
