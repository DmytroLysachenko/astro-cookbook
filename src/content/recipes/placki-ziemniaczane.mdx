## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes

Peel and grate the **potatoes** and **onion**. Squeeze out any excess liquid using a clean kitchen towel.

<CookingStep action="cutting" />

### Step 2: Prepare the Batter

In a bowl, mix the grated potatoes and onions with **eggs, flour, salt, and pepper**.

<CookingStep action="mixing" />

### Step 3: Fry the Pancakes

Heat **vegetable oil** in a pan. Drop spoonfuls of the potato mixture into the hot oil and flatten them into pancakes. Fry until golden brown on both sides.

<CookingStep action="frying" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the potato pancakes hot with **sour cream** or **applesauce**.
