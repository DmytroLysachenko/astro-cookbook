## Introduction

Placki Ziemniaczane, or **Polish Potato Pancakes**, are **crispy and golden** pancakes made with grated potatoes, onions, and flour. They are **quick to prepare** and are often served with **sour cream** or **applesauce**. These pancakes are a **comforting snack** or side dish that’s perfect for any occasion.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Potatoes

Peel and grate the **potatoes** and **onion**. Squeeze out any excess liquid using a clean kitchen towel.

<CookingStep action="cutting_vegetables_potato" />

### Step 2: Prepare the Batter

In a bowl, mix the grated potatoes and onions with **eggs, flour, salt, and pepper**.

<CookingStep action="mixing_bowl_liquids_batter" />

### Step 3: Fry the Pancakes

Heat **vegetable oil** in a pan. Drop spoonfuls of the potato mixture into the hot oil and flatten them into pancakes. Fry until golden brown on both sides.

<CookingStep action="frying_pan_substance_creamy" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the potato pancakes hot with **sour cream** or **applesauce**.

## Final Thoughts

Placki Ziemniaczane are a **crispy and comforting dish** that’s perfect for a quick snack or side dish. The combination of **grated potatoes and onions** creates a flavorful pancake that’s sure to please. Enjoy this traditional Polish dish with your family and friends!
