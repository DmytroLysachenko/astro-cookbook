## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Cabbage

Boil the **whole cabbage** in a large pot of water for **10 minutes**, then remove the leaves carefully. Trim the thick stems from the leaves.

<CookingStep action="boiling" />

### Step 2: Prepare the Filling

In a bowl, mix **ground pork, cooked rice, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika**.

<CookingStep action="mixing" />

### Step 3: Assemble the Rolls

Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each cabbage leaf, then roll the leaf around the filling, tucking in the sides.

<CookingStep action="cutting" />

### Step 4: Bake the Rolls

Preheat the oven to **180¬∞C (350¬∞F)**. Place the cabbage rolls in a baking dish and pour **tomato sauce** over them. Cover with foil and bake for **1 hour**.

<CookingStep action="oven" />

### Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the Go≈ÇƒÖbki hot with **sour cream** on the side.
