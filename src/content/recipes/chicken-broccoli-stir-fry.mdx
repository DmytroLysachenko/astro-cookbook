## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Slice the **chicken breast** into thin strips. Cut the **broccoli** into florets. Mince the **garlic**.

<CookingStep action="cutting-meat-poultry-breasts" />

### Step 2: Cook the Stir-Fry

Heat the **olive oil** in a pan and sauté the **garlic** until fragrant. Add the **chicken** and cook until browned. Add the **broccoli** and **soy sauce**, and stir-fry until the broccoli is tender. Season with **salt** and **black pepper**.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-meat-poultry-with-veggies" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the stir-fry hot with rice or noodles.
