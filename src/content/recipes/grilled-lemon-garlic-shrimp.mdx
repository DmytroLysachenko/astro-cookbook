## Introduction

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp is a **quick and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a summer barbecue or a light dinner. The shrimp are marinated in a **zesty lemon garlic sauce** and grilled to perfection, making them juicy and full of flavor.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Marinade

In a bowl, mix **olive oil**, **minced garlic**, **lemon juice**, **chopped parsley**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Stir well to combine.

<CookingStep
  action="mixing-bowl-liquids-marinade"
  alt="Mixing marinade in a bowl"
/>

### Step 2: Marinate the Shrimp

Add the **shrimp** to the marinade and toss to coat. Let it sit for **15 minutes** to absorb the flavors.

<CookingStep action="marinating-shrimps" alt="Marinating shrimp" />

### Step 3: Grill the Shrimp

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the shrimp onto skewers and grill for **2-3 minutes per side** until they turn pink and are cooked through.

<CookingStep action="grilling-fish-shrimp" alt="Grilling shrimp on skewers" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the grilled shrimp hot with a side of **lemon wedges** and **fresh parsley**. Perfect with a side of rice or a fresh salad.

## Final Thoughts

Grilled Lemon Garlic Shrimp is a **simple yet delicious dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of **zesty lemon** and **garlic** makes the shrimp incredibly flavorful. Enjoy this dish with family and friends!
