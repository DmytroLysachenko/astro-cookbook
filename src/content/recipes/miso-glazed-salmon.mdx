## Introduction

Miso Glazed Salmon is a **Japanese-inspired dish** that combines the rich flavors of miso, soy sauce, and honey to create a sweet and savory glaze for tender salmon fillets. This dish is **quick to prepare** and pairs perfectly with steamed rice and vegetables.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Miso Glaze

In a small bowl, mix together **miso paste, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger**. Stir until well combined.

<CookingStep action="mixing" />

### Step 2: Marinate the Salmon

Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the miso glaze over them. Let the salmon marinate for **15-20 minutes**.

<CookingStep action="wait" />

### Step 3: Cook the Salmon

Preheat the oven to **200°C (400°F)**. Place the marinated salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for **10-12 minutes**, or until the salmon is cooked through and the glaze is caramelized.

<CookingStep action="oven" />

### Step 4: Garnish and Serve

Sprinkle the cooked salmon with **sesame seeds** and **chopped green onions**. Serve with **steamed rice** and **steamed vegetables** for a complete meal.

## Final Thoughts

Miso Glazed Salmon is a **flavorful and healthy dish** that’s perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. The combination of **sweet and savory flavors** makes this dish a favorite among seafood lovers. Enjoy this Japanese-inspired meal with your family and friends!
