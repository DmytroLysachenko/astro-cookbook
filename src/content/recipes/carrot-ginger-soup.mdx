## Introduction

This **Carrot and Ginger Soup** is a **warm and comforting dish** perfect for chilly days. The combination of **sweet carrots** and **spicy ginger** creates a flavorful and nutritious soup that’s easy to make.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Peel and chop the **carrots**, **onion**, and **ginger**.

<CookingStep action="cutting-vegetables-carrot-onion-ginger" />

### Step 2: Cook the Soup

In a pot, heat the **olive oil** and sauté the **onion** and **ginger** until soft. Add the **carrots** and **vegetable stock**, and simmer until the carrots are tender. Blend the soup until smooth, then stir in the **cream**. Season with **salt** and **black pepper**.

<CookingStep action="stirring-soupPot-curry-soup" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the soup hot, garnished with a drizzle of cream or fresh herbs.

## Final Thoughts

This **Carrot and Ginger Soup** is a **hearty and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of **carrots and ginger** adds a unique twist to a classic soup. Enjoy this comforting recipe with your family and friends!
