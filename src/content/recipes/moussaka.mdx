## Introduction

Moussaka is a **traditional Greek casserole** made with layers of eggplant, ground lamb, and a creamy béchamel sauce. This dish is **rich and flavorful**, perfect for a hearty dinner. Enjoy this classic Greek recipe with your family and friends!

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Eggplant

Slice the **eggplant** into 1cm thick slices. Sprinkle with **salt** and let sit for 30 minutes to remove bitterness. Rinse and pat dry.

<CookingStep action="cutting-vegetables-eggplant" alt="Cutting eggplant" />

### Step 2: Cook the Lamb

In a large pan, heat **olive oil** and sauté **chopped onions** and **minced garlic** until softened. Add **ground lamb** and cook until browned. Stir in **tomato puree**, **salt**, and **pepper**. Simmer for 10 minutes.

<CookingStep action="lamb-onions" alt="Cooking ground lamb" />

### Step 3: Prepare the Béchamel Sauce

In a saucepan, melt **butter** and stir in **flour** to make a roux. Gradually add **milk**, stirring constantly until thickened. Season with **nutmeg**, **salt**, and **pepper**.

<CookingStep action="stirring-saucePan-cream" alt="Preparing béchamel sauce" />

### Step 4: Assemble the Moussaka

Layer the **eggplant slices**, **lamb mixture**, and **béchamel sauce** in a baking dish. Repeat layers, finishing with béchamel on top.

<CookingStep action="assembling-moussaka" alt="Assembling moussaka" />

### Step 5: Bake & Serve

Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 45 minutes, or until golden brown. Let cool slightly before serving.

## Final Thoughts

Moussaka is a **rich and flavorful casserole** that’s perfect for a hearty dinner. The combination of **eggplant, lamb, and béchamel sauce** creates a satisfying dish. Enjoy this traditional Greek recipe with your family and friends!
