## Introduction

These **Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos** are a **delicious and healthy vegetarian option** that’s perfect for a quick dinner. The combination of **roasted sweet potatoes**, **black beans**, and a **zesty lime dressing** makes these tacos both flavorful and satisfying.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Peel and dice the **sweet potatoes**. Drain and rinse the **black beans**.

<CookingStep action="cutting-vegetables-potato" />

### Step 2: Roast the Sweet Potatoes

Toss the **sweet potatoes** with **olive oil**, **cumin**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Roast in the oven until tender.

<CookingStep action="roasting-oven-vegetablesMix" />

### Step 3: Assemble the Tacos

Warm the **tortillas** and fill them with the roasted **sweet potatoes** and **black beans**. Drizzle with **lime juice**.

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the tacos immediately with your favorite toppings.

## Final Thoughts

These **Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos** are a **nutritious and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a quick and easy dinner. The combination of **sweet potatoes and black beans** makes it a satisfying vegetarian option. Enjoy this recipe with your family and friends!
