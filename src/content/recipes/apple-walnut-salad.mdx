## Introduction

This **Apple and Walnut Salad** is a **light and refreshing dish** perfect for a quick lunch or a side dish. The combination of **crisp apples, crunchy walnuts, and fresh lettuce** creates a delightful texture, while the **simple vinaigrette** ties everything together.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Wash and chop the **lettuce**. Core and slice the **apples**. Roughly chop the **walnuts**.

<CookingStep action="cutting_vegetables_lettuce" />

### Step 2: Make the Vinaigrette

In a small bowl, whisk together the **olive oil**, **lemon juice**, **salt**, and **black pepper**.

<CookingStep action="mixing_bowl_liquids_marinade" />

### Step 3: Assemble the Salad

In a large bowl, combine the **lettuce**, **apple slices**, and **walnuts**. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss gently to coat.

<CookingStep action="mixing_bowl_vegetables_salad" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the salad immediately as a light meal or a side dish.
