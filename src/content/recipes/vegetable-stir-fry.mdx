## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Cut the **broccoli**, **carrots**, **bell peppers**, and **snow peas** into bite-sized pieces.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-vegetables-mix-broccoli-carrot-pepper"
  alt="Cutting vegetables"
/>

### Step 2: Sauté the Aromatics

Heat **vegetable oil** in a large pan or wok. Add **minced garlic** and **grated ginger**. Sauté until fragrant.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-pan-vegetables-onions"
  alt="Sautéing garlic and ginger"
/>

### Step 3: Stir-Fry the Vegetables

Add the **prepared vegetables** to the pan. Stir-fry for **5-7 minutes** until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.

<CookingStep action="stirring-pan-vegetables" alt="Stir-frying vegetables" />

### Step 4: Add the Sauce

Add **soy sauce**, **sesame oil**, **salt**, and **black pepper** to the pan. Stir well to coat the vegetables with the sauce.

<CookingStep
  action="seasoning-spices-black"
  alt="Adding sauce to the vegetables"
/>

### Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the vegetable stir-fry hot with **steamed rice** or **noodles**.

## Final Thoughts

This **vegetable stir-fry** is a **quick and healthy dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The **mix of fresh vegetables** and **savory sauce** makes this dish both delicious and nutritious. Enjoy this dish with your family and friends!
