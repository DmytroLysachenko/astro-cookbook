## Introduction

**Shrimp Stir Fry** is a **quick and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a weeknight dinner. Made with fresh shrimp, vegetables, and a savory sauce, this stir fry is both healthy and delicious.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Peel and devein the **shrimp**. Chop the **broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot** into bite-sized pieces. Mince the **garlic** and grate the **ginger**.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-fish-shrimps"
  alt="Preparing shrimp and vegetables"
/>

### Step 2: Cook the Shrimp

Heat **sesame oil** in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the **shrimp** and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-fish-shrimps" alt="Cooking shrimp in a pan" />

### Step 3: Stir Fry the Vegetables

In the same pan, add the **garlic and ginger** and sauté until fragrant. Add the **broccoli, bell pepper, and carrot** and stir fry for **5-7 minutes**, or until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-shrimp-veggies"
  alt="Stir frying vegetables in a pan"
/>

### Step 4: Combine and Serve

Return the shrimp to the pan. In a small bowl, mix the **soy sauce, cornstarch, and water** to create a sauce. Pour the sauce over the shrimp and vegetables and cook until the sauce thickens. Serve hot with rice or noodles.

## Final Thoughts

This **Shrimp Stir Fry** is a **quick and healthy meal** that’s packed with flavor. The combination of fresh shrimp, vegetables, and a savory sauce makes this dish a family favorite. Enjoy!
