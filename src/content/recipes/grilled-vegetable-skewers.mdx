## Introduction

Grilled Vegetable Skewers are a **healthy and vibrant dish** that’s perfect for a summer barbecue. The vegetables are marinated in a **tangy balsamic glaze** and grilled to perfection, making them a delicious and colorful addition to any meal.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Cut the **bell peppers**, **zucchini**, and **red onion** into bite-sized pieces. Thread the vegetables and **cherry tomatoes** onto skewers.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-vegetables-mix-tomato-onion-green"
  alt="Cutting vegetables for skewers"
/>

### Step 2: Prepare the Marinade

In a bowl, mix **olive oil**, **balsamic vinegar**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Stir well to combine.

<CookingStep
  action="mixing-bowl-liquids-marinade"
  alt="Mixing marinade in a bowl"
/>

### Step 3: Marinate the Vegetables

Brush the marinade over the vegetable skewers and let them sit for **10 minutes** to absorb the flavors.

<CookingStep
  action="seasoning-coating-red-meat-medalions"
  alt="Marinating vegetable skewers"
/>

### Step 4: Grill the Skewers

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Grill the vegetable skewers for **5-7 minutes per side** until tender and slightly charred.

<CookingStep
  action="grilling-skewers-veggie"
  alt="Grilling vegetable skewers"
/>

### Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the grilled vegetable skewers hot with a side of **grilled bread** or **quinoa**. Perfect for a light and healthy meal!

## Final Thoughts

Grilled Vegetable Skewers are a **delicious and healthy dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of **balsamic glaze** and **grilled vegetables** makes them incredibly flavorful. Enjoy this dish with family and friends!
