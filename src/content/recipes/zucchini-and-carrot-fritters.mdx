## Introduction

These **Zucchini and Carrot Fritters** are a **crispy and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a quick and healthy meal. The combination of **grated zucchini** and **carrots** makes these fritters both nutritious and delicious.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Grate the **zucchini** and **carrots**. Squeeze out any excess moisture.

<CookingStep action="kitchenAppliances-grater" alt="Grate ingredients" />

### Step 2: Mix the Batter

In a bowl, combine the grated **zucchini**, **carrots**, **eggs**, **flour**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Mix until well combined.

<CookingStep action="mixing-bowl-vegetables-stirFry" alt="Mix ingredients" />

### Step 3: Cook the Fritters

Heat the **olive oil** in a pan and spoon the batter into the pan to form fritters. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-dumplings" alt="Frying Fritters" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the fritters hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

## Final Thoughts

These **Zucchini and Carrot Fritters** are a **quick and healthy dish** that’s perfect for a light meal or snack. The combination of **zucchini and carrots** makes them both nutritious and flavorful. Enjoy this recipe with your family and friends!
