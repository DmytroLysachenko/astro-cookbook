## Introduction

Risotto ai Funghi is a **classic Italian dish** that features creamy Arborio rice cooked with mushrooms, white wine, and Parmesan cheese. This dish is **comforting and flavorful**, making it perfect for a cozy dinner.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Mushrooms

Heat **olive oil** in a pan and sauté **sliced mushrooms** until golden brown. Season with **salt and pepper** and set aside.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-vegetables-mushrooms-onion" />

### Step 2: Cook the Onion and Garlic

In a large pot, melt **butter** and sauté **chopped onion and minced garlic** until softened.

<CookingStep action="pot-onions" />

### Step 3: Toast the Rice

Add the **Arborio rice** to the pot and toast for **2-3 minutes**, stirring constantly.

<CookingStep action="stirring-pan-rice" />

### Step 4: Add Wine and Stock

Pour in **white wine** and cook until absorbed. Gradually add **vegetable stock**, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until the rice is creamy and cooked through.

<CookingStep action="stirring-pan-rice-veggies" />

### Step 5: Add Mushrooms and Cheese

Stir in the cooked mushrooms and **grated Parmesan cheese**. Season with **salt and pepper** to taste.

### Step 6: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the risotto hot, garnished with **extra Parmesan** and **chopped parsley**.

## Final Thoughts

Risotto ai Funghi is a **creamy and comforting dish** that’s perfect for a cozy dinner. The combination of **mushrooms, Parmesan, and Arborio rice** creates a rich and flavorful meal that’s sure to impress. Enjoy this classic Italian dish with your family and friends!
