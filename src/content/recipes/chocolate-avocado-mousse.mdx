## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Peel and pit the **avocados**.

### Step 2: Blend the Mousse

In a blender, combine the **avocados**, **cocoa powder**, **honey**, **milk**, and **vanilla extract**. Blend until smooth and creamy.

<CookingStep action="kitchenAppliances_hand_blender" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Spoon the mousse into serving dishes and chill before serving.
