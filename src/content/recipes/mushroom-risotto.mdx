## Introduction

**Mushroom Risotto** is a **creamy and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a comforting dinner. Made with Arborio rice, mushrooms, and Parmesan cheese, this risotto is both rich and satisfying.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Chop the **onion** and **garlic**. Slice the **mushrooms**. Grate the **Parmesan cheese**.

<CookingStep
  action="chopping-onion-cheese"
  alt="Sautéing mushrooms and onions in a pan"
/>

### Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

In a large pan, heat **olive oil** and **butter** over medium heat. Add the **onion and garlic** and sauté until softened. Add the **mushrooms** and cook until they release their moisture and become golden.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-pan-vegetables-mushrooms-onion"
  alt="Sautéing mushrooms and onions in a pan"
/>

### Step 3: Cook the Rice

Add the **Arborio rice** to the pan and stir to coat the grains with the oil and butter. Pour in the **white wine** and cook until it has evaporated. Gradually add the **vegetable stock**, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly until the rice is creamy and cooked through.

<CookingStep action="pan-rice-vegetables" alt="Stirring risotto in a pan" />

### Step 4: Finish and Serve

Stir in the **Parmesan cheese** and season with **salt and pepper**. Serve the risotto hot, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.

## Final Thoughts

This **Mushroom Risotto** is a **rich and comforting dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The creamy texture and earthy flavor of the mushrooms make this risotto a favorite. Enjoy!
