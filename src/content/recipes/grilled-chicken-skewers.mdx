## Introduction

Grilled Chicken Skewers are a **classic barbecue dish** that’s perfect for any gathering. The chicken is marinated in a **savory herb sauce** and grilled to juicy perfection. Serve with your favorite sides for a complete meal.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Marinade

In a bowl, mix **olive oil**, **minced garlic**, **lemon juice**, **rosemary**, **thyme**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Stir well to combine.

<CookingStep
  action="mixing-bowl-liquids-marinade"
  alt="Mixing marinade in a bowl"
/>

### Step 2: Marinate the Chicken

Cut the **chicken breast** into cubes and add them to the marinade. Toss to coat and let it sit for **30 minutes** to absorb the flavors.

<CookingStep
  action="waiting-marinating-meat-chicken-medalions"
  alt="Marinating chicken"
/>

### Step 3: Grill the Skewers

Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Thread the chicken onto skewers and grill for **5-7 minutes per side** until fully cooked.

<CookingStep action="grilling-skewers-chicken" alt="Grilling chicken skewers" />

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the grilled chicken skewers hot with a side of **grilled vegetables** or **rice**. Perfect for a summer barbecue!

## Final Thoughts

Grilled Chicken Skewers are a **crowd-pleasing dish** that’s easy to prepare and full of flavor. The combination of **herbs** and **garlic** makes the chicken incredibly tasty. Enjoy this dish with family and friends!
