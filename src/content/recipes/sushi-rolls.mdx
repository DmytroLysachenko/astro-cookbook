## Introduction

Sushi Rolls are a **classic Japanese dish** made with vinegared rice, nori, and various fillings like fish and vegetables. This dish is **fresh and flavorful**, perfect for a light dinner or snack. Enjoy this traditional Japanese recipe with your family and friends!

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Sushi Rice

Rinse the **sushi rice** until the water runs clear. Cook according to package instructions. Mix **rice vinegar**, **sugar**, and **salt** in a small bowl, then fold into the cooked rice.

<CookingStep action="boiling-grains-rice" alt="Cooking sushi rice" />

### Step 2: Prepare the Fillings

Slice the **salmon**, **cucumber**, and **avocado** into thin strips.

<CookingStep action="cutting-avocado-fish-cucumber" alt="Cutting salmon" />

### Step 3: Assemble the Sushi Rolls

Place a **nori sheet** on a bamboo mat. Spread a thin layer of **sushi rice** over the nori, leaving a 1cm border at the top. Arrange the **salmon**, **cucumber**, and **avocado** in the center. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat.

<CookingStep action="arranging-sushi" alt="Arranging sushi" />

### Step 4: Slice and Serve

Slice the sushi roll into 6-8 pieces using a sharp knife. Serve with **soy sauce** and enjoy!
