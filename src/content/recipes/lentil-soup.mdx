## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Chop the **carrot, celery, onion, and garlic**.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-vegetables-mix-tomato-onion-green"
  alt="Chopping vegetables for lentil soup"
/>

### Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

In a large pot, heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add the **onion, garlic, carrot, and celery** and sauté until softened. Stir in the **cumin and paprika**.

<CookingStep action="large-pot-vegetables" alt="Sautéing vegetables in a pot" />

### Step 3: Cook the Lentils

Add the **lentils, tomato puree, and vegetable stock** to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for **30 minutes**, or until the lentils are tender.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring-soupPot-red-soup"
  alt="Simmering lentil soup in a pot"
/>

### Step 4: Season and Serve

Season the soup with **salt and pepper**. Serve hot with crusty bread.

## Final Thoughts

This **Lentil Soup** is a **nutritious and comforting dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of lentils, vegetables, and spices makes this soup a family favorite. Enjoy!
