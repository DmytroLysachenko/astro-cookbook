## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Chop the **tomatoes** and **basil**. Slice the **baguette** and toast it. Mince the **garlic**.

<CookingStep action="cutting-bread-tomato-garlic" />

### Step 2: Assemble the Bruschetta

In a bowl, mix the **tomatoes**, **basil**, **olive oil**, **garlic**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Spoon the mixture onto the toasted **baguette slices**.

<CookingStep action="baguette-tomatos" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the bruschetta immediately as an appetizer or snack.
