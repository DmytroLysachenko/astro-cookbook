## Introduction

Butter Chicken, also known as **Murgh Makhani**, is a **classic Indian dish** that features tender chicken in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. This dish is **flavorful and comforting**, making it a favorite among curry lovers. Serve it with **naan** or **steamed rice** for a complete meal.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, mix together **yogurt, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, cayenne pepper, and salt**. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for **30 minutes**.

<CookingStep action="mix-white-marinade" />

### Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Heat a pan with **1 tablespoon of butter** and cook the marinated chicken until browned on all sides. Remove the chicken and set aside.

<CookingStep action="frying-pan-meat-poultry-cubes" />

### Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

In the same pan, add **remaining butter, minced garlic, and grated ginger**. Sauté until fragrant. Add **tomato puree** and cook for **5 minutes**. Stir in **heavy cream** and bring to a simmer.

<CookingStep action="stirring-saucePan-tomato" />

### Step 4: Combine Chicken and Sauce

Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for **10-15 minutes**, until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is thickened.

<CookingStep action="stewing-poultry-tomato" />

### Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Garnish the Butter Chicken with **chopped cilantro** and serve with **naan** or **steamed rice**.

## Final Thoughts

Butter Chicken is a **rich and creamy dish** that’s perfect for a comforting dinner. The combination of **spices, tomatoes, and cream** creates a flavorful sauce that pairs perfectly with tender chicken. Enjoy this classic Indian dish with your family and friends!
