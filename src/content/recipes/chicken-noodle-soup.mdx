**Chicken Noodle Soup** is a **comforting and classic dish** that’s perfect for a hearty meal. Made with tender chicken, vegetables, and egg noodles, this soup is both nourishing and delicious.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Chop the **carrot, celery, onion, and garlic**.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting_vegetables_mix_tomato_onion_green"
  alt="Chopping vegetables for soup"
/>

### Step 2: Cook the Chicken

In a large pot, heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add the **chicken breast** and cook until browned on both sides. Remove the chicken and set aside.

<CookingStep
  action="frying_pan_meat_poultry_breast"
  alt="Cooking chicken breast in a pot"
/>

### Step 3: Sauté the Vegetables

In the same pot, add the **onion, garlic, carrot, and celery** and sauté until softened. Add the **thyme, salt, and pepper**.

<CookingStep
  action="frying_pan_vegetables_onions"
  alt="Sautéing vegetables in a pot"
/>

### Step 4: Simmer the Soup

Return the chicken to the pot. Add the **chicken stock** and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for **20 minutes**. Remove the chicken, shred it, and return it to the pot.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring_soupPot_white_soup"
  alt="Simmering chicken noodle soup in a pot"
/>

### Step 5: Add the Noodles

Add the **egg noodles** to the pot and cook until tender, about **8-10 minutes**.

<CookingStep action="boiling_pasta_long" alt="Boiling noodles in soup" />

### Step 6: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring_soupPot_white"
  alt="Serving chicken noodle soup in a bowl"
/>

## Final Thoughts

This **Chicken Noodle Soup** is a **nourishing and comforting dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of tender chicken, vegetables, and egg noodles makes this soup a family favorite. Enjoy!
