## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Patties

Season the **ground beef** with **salt and pepper**. Divide the beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.

<CookingStep action="tendering_meat_red_medalions" alt="Shaping beef patties" />

### Step 2: Cook the Patties

Heat a grill or frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook the beef patties for about **4-5 minutes on each side**, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Add a slice of **cheese** to each patty during the last minute of cooking.

<CookingStep action="grilling_meat_red_burgers" alt="Grilling beef patties" />

### Step 3: Assemble the Burgers

Toast the **burger buns** lightly. Spread **ketchup and mustard** on the bottom bun. Layer with **lettuce, tomato slices, onion rings, pickles**, and the cooked beef patty. Top with the other half of the bun.

### Step 4: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the burgers immediately with your favorite sides, such as fries or a salad.

## Final Thoughts

This **Beef Burger** is a **classic and satisfying meal** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of juicy beef, fresh vegetables, and a soft bun makes this burger a crowd-pleaser. Enjoy!
