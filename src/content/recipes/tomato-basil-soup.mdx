## Introduction

**Tomato Basil Soup** is a **classic and comforting dish** that’s perfect for a cozy meal. Made with ripe tomatoes, fresh basil, and a touch of cream, this soup is both rich and flavorful.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Chop the **tomatoes, onion, and garlic**.

<CookingStep
  action="cutting-vegetables-tomato-onion-garlic"
  alt="Chopping tomatoes and vegetables"
/>

### Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

In a large pot, heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add the **onion and garlic** and sauté until softened. Add the **tomatoes** and cook until they start to break down.

<CookingStep
  action="pan-vegetables-tomato-onion-garlic"
  alt="Sautéing tomatoes and onions in a pot"
/>

### Step 3: Simmer the Soup

Add the **vegetable stock** and **basil** to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for **20 minutes**.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring-soupPot-red-soup"
  alt="Simmering tomato soup in a pot"
/>

### Step 4: Blend and Finish

Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Stir in the **heavy cream** and season with **salt and pepper**.

<CookingStep
  action="kitchenAppliances-hand-blender"
  alt="Blending tomato soup"
/>

### Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.

## Final Thoughts

This **Tomato Basil Soup** is a **rich and comforting dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of ripe tomatoes, fresh basil, and cream makes this soup a family favorite. Enjoy!
