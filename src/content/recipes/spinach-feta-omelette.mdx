## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Wash and chop the **spinach**. Crumble the **feta cheese**.

<CookingStep action="spinach-feta" />

### Step 2: Cook the Omelette

In a bowl, whisk the **eggs** with **salt** and **black pepper**. Heat the **olive oil** in a pan, add the **spinach**, and cook until wilted. Pour the eggs over the spinach and sprinkle with **feta cheese**. Cook until the eggs are set.

<CookingStep action="frying-omlette-spinach" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Fold the omelette and serve immediately.

This **Spinach and Feta Omelette** is a **simple yet flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a quick breakfast or brunch. The combination of **spinach and feta** adds a Mediterranean twist to a classic omelette. Enjoy this easy recipe with your family and friends!
