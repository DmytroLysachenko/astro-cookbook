## Introduction

**Butternut Squash Soup** is a **creamy and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a cozy meal. Made with roasted butternut squash, spices, and a touch of cream, this soup is both rich and satisfying.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Roast the Squash

Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F). Peel and cube the **butternut squash**. Toss the squash with **olive oil, salt, and pepper**. Roast in the oven for **30 minutes**, or until tender.

<CookingStep
  action="roasting-oven-vegetablesMix"
  alt="Roasting butternut squash in the oven"
/>

### Step 2: Sauté the Vegetables

In a large pot, heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add the **onion and garlic** and sauté until softened. Stir in the **cumin and paprika**.

<CookingStep
  action="frying-pan-vegetables-onions"
  alt="Sautéing onions and garlic in a pot"
/>

### Step 3: Simmer the Soup

Add the **roasted butternut squash** and **vegetable stock** to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for **15 minutes**.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring-soupPot-red-soup"
  alt="Simmering butternut squash soup in a pot"
/>

### Step 4: Blend and Finish

Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Stir in the **heavy cream** and season with **salt and pepper**.

<CookingStep
  action="kitchenAppliances-hand-blender"
  alt="Blending butternut squash soup"
/>

### Step 5: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the soup hot, garnished with a drizzle of cream and a sprinkle of paprika.

<CookingStep
  action="stirring-soupPot-red"
  alt="Serving butternut squash soup in a bowl"
/>

## Final Thoughts

This **Butternut Squash Soup** is a **rich and comforting dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of roasted squash, spices, and cream makes this soup a family favorite. Enjoy!
