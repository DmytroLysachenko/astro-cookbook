## Introduction

Thai Green Curry is a **fragrant and spicy dish** that combines tender chicken, green curry paste, and coconut milk with fresh vegetables. This dish is **flavorful and aromatic**, making it a favorite among Thai cuisine lovers. Serve it with **jasmine rice** for a complete meal.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Cook the Chicken

Heat a pan with **1 tablespoon of oil** and cook the chicken pieces until browned. Remove and set aside.

<CookingStep action="frying" />

### Step 2: Prepare the Curry

In the same pan, add **green curry paste** and cook for **1-2 minutes** until fragrant. Stir in **coconut milk** and **vegetable stock**.

<CookingStep action="stewing" />

### Step 3: Add Vegetables

Add **sliced bell pepper** and **zucchini** to the curry and simmer until the vegetables are tender.

<CookingStep action="stewing" />

### Step 4: Add Chicken and Seasoning

Return the cooked chicken to the pan and stir in **fish sauce** and **lime juice**. Simmer for **5 minutes**.

<CookingStep action="stewing" />

### Step 5: Garnish and Serve

Garnish the curry with **Thai basil** and serve with **jasmine rice**.
