## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Chop the **carrots**, **potatoes**, **tomatoes**, **onion**, and **garlic**.

<CookingStep action="cutting_vegetables_mix_tomato_eggplnt_carrot" />

### Step 2: Cook the Curry

In a pot, heat the **olive oil** and sauté the **onion** and **garlic** until soft. Add the **curry powder** and stir. Add the **lentils**, **carrots**, **potatoes**, **tomatoes**, and **coconut milk**. Simmer until the lentils and vegetables are tender. Season with **salt** and **black pepper**.

<CookingStep action="stirring_soupPot_curry" />

### Step 3: Serve & Enjoy!

Serve the curry hot with rice or naan bread.

## Final Thoughts

This **Lentil and Vegetable Curry** is a **nutritious and flavorful dish** that’s perfect for a comforting meal. The combination of **lentils and vegetables** makes it a satisfying vegetarian option. Enjoy this recipe with your family and friends!
