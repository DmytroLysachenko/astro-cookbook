## Introduction

**Grilled Chicken Salad** is a **fresh and healthy dish** that’s perfect for a light lunch or dinner. Made with grilled chicken, mixed greens, and a tangy vinaigrette, this salad is both nutritious and delicious.

## Step-by-Step Cooking Guide

### Step 1: Grill the Chicken

Season the **chicken breast** with **salt and pepper**. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat until cooked through, about **6-8 minutes per side**. Let it rest before slicing.

<CookingStep
  action="grilling-meat-poultry-breast"
  alt="Grilling chicken breast"
/>

### Step 2: Prepare the Salad

Chop the **mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion**. Toss them together in a large bowl.

<CookingStep
  action="salad-ingredients-bowl"
  alt="Preparing salad ingredients"
/>

### Step 3: Make the Vinaigrette

In a small bowl, whisk together the **olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper**.

<CookingStep
  action="mixing-bowl-liquids-honey-sauce"
  alt="Whisking vinaigrette in a bowl"
/>

### Step 4: Assemble and Serve

Slice the grilled chicken and place it on top of the salad. Drizzle with the vinaigrette and serve immediately.

## Final Thoughts

This **Grilled Chicken Salad** is a **light and refreshing dish** that’s perfect for any occasion. The combination of grilled chicken, fresh vegetables, and a tangy vinaigrette makes this salad a favorite. Enjoy!
